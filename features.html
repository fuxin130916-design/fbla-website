Aircraft Collection

On Display

OH-6A Cayuse

First flying in 1963, publicly introduces 1966

Formerly the Hughes Model 369, the OH-6A Cayuse (nicknamed “Loach”) was designed for military scouting during the Vietnam conflict.

AH-1S Cobra

Introduced in 1967

The Bell AH-1 Cobra is a single-engine attack helicopter developed using components from the UH-1 Iroquois.

HH-1K Huey

Introduced in 1964

27 HH-1K Huey aircraft were produced primarily as US Navy sea/air rescue helicopters.

HH-43 Huskie

introduced in 1957

Used throughout the 1950s to 1970s as a firefighting and rescue aircraft by the United States Air Force, Navy and Marine Corps.
